How to Play!

Hello and welcome to the Super Sci-Fi Quiz. The instructions are simple...


Once you press start you'll be asked 10 questions rellating to the sci-fi genre. There will be no time limit, once selected the option you selected will either go green if correct, or red if the answer was incorrect. You won't be able to change your answer.


When answered you'll then need to click on the next button which will take you to the next question. There will be a scoreboard at the end which will let you know how many points you scored, you'll get 1 point for each question you answer correct. Good luck!
